Explanation

In the quiz creation tool, every single text box has an explanation right under it to help you understand what it does. If, however, you are more of a visual learner, the images on the left and right should explain exactly what each of the text boxes do. Every text box has a label and on our images, each label is included! There is even more visible in the code.
